Privacy Policy (Demo)

This website is a demo. It describes how user data is processed for the features on this static site.

Local notes & ratings

Notes and ratings saved via the on-site form are stored in your browser's localStorage by default. They are not sent to any server unless you enable Firebase and provide configuration.

Advertising

If you integrate an ad network (e.g., Google AdSense), third-party providers may set cookies and collect personal data according to their policies. You must follow those providers' terms and display their required notices. This demo provides placeholder ad slots only.

Firebase

If you configure Firebase to enable persistent comments, data will be stored in your Firebase project. You are responsible for ensuring your Firebase rules and privacy practices comply with GDPR and other applicable laws.
